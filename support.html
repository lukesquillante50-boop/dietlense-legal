Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → DietLens → Cancel.

How do I delete my account?
In DietLens, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your data.

Are the nutrition numbers exact?
They're AI and database estimates meant to guide you, not medical advice. Always consult a professional for dietary decisions.
